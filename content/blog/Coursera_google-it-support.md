I completed the **[Coursera Google IT Support Professional Certificate](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/google-it-support)** (8-months - 10hrs/week) program.

This five-course certificate, developed by [Grow with Google](https://www.blog.google/outreach-initiatives/grow-with-google/it-support-professional-certificate/), includes innovative curriculum designed to improve IT specialist role. A job in IT mean in-person or remote helpdesk work.

Through a mix of 64 hours of video lessons, lectures, quizzes, and hand-on labs and widgets, the program introduced me to troubleshooting and customer service, networking, operating systems, system administration and best practices about state-of-the-art of IT security. Along the way, I head from Googlers with unique backgrounds whose own foundation in IT specialist served as a jumping off point for their careers.
