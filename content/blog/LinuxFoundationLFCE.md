I completed the **[Linux Foundation LFCE (Linux Foundation Certified Engineer)](https://training.linuxfoundation.org/certification/lfce)** certification!

* [Linux Foundation Certified Engineer certificate](pdf/LinuxFoundation_LFCE_certificate.pdf) (passed on 3<sup>rd</sup> December 2017)
* [Linux Foundation Linux Networking and Administration (LFS211) certificate](pdf/LinuxFoundation_linux-networking-and-administration-lfs211-certificate.pdf) (passed on 25<sup>th</sup> January 2017)

The [Linux Foundation](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/) is the foundation
that support the development of Linux and others Open Source initiatives worldwide.
The Linux creator "[Linus Torvalds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linus_Torvalds)"
works under its umbrella and hosts [kernel.org](https://www.kernel.org/) website.


A
[LFCE (Linux Foundation Certified Engineer)](https://training.linuxfoundation.org/certification/lfce)
certified engineer possesses in depth skills of Linux system and is responsible
for the design and implementation of system architecture.
