A lot of hard work has been done under the hood to improve this website:

* **automation** with [Gulp](https://gulpjs.com/) pipeline for minify and publication
* **off-line support** using [Workbox Service Worker](https://developers.google.com/web/tools/workbox/)
* **secure** thanks to [DNSSEC](http://dnssec-debugger.verisignlabs.com/mrevolution.eu) support, [Let's Encrypt](https://letsencrypt.org/) certificate, [HTTP/2](https://developers.google.com/web/fundamentals/performance/http2/) support and [HTTP Strict Transport Security](https://hstspreload.org/?domain=mrevolution.eu) technology (via [CloudFlare](https://www.cloudflare.com/))
