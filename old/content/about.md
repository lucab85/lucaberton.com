## Automation Strategy Consulting
I specialize in crafting efficient automation strategies tailored to your unique needs. From designing CI/CD pipelines that reduce error rates to integrating machine learning models into your workflows, I focus on enhancing your infrastructure's performance. Whether you're operating in a hybrid or multi-cloud environment, I provide Infrastructure as Code (IaC) solutions that streamline your operations.

## Kubernetes, Containerization, & RAG
As an expert in container orchestration and management, I offer comprehensive workshops on Kubernetes, Docker, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). These hands-on sessions are designed to equip your team with the skills needed to replatform applications to Kubernetes/OpenShift, enhancing scalability and reducing the carbon footprint of your operations.

## Cloud Infrastructure, AI Integration & Green Code
My services include designing and optimizing cloud architectures for AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud with a focus on energy efficiency. I also integrate security, compliance, and AI-driven solutions into your cloud environments, all while adhering to green coding practices that promote sustainability.

## Ansible, Python & Green Code Training
I provide in-depth training on Ansible for automation, configuration management, and orchestration. My Python-based automation workshops are tailored to integrate machine learning models with Ansible workflows, offering customized sessions for automating complex tasks in VMware, Kubernetes, and enterprise environments.

## Performance Optimization, Custom Solutions & Green Code
My expertise extends to profiling and optimizing Ansible playbooks, Python scripts, and infrastructure for better performance and energy efficiency. I fine-tune cloud and on-premises setups to ensure cost efficiency, speed, and minimal environmental impact.
