## 🇬🇧 Ansible London Meetup – September 23, 2024

Yesterday’s **Ansible London Meetup** was full of open-source energy and community connections! I had the pleasure of demoing how to deploy **GraphRAG** using **Ansible** — integrating the power of **OpenAI’s ChatGPT LLM** with the **Neo4j** graph database.

The room was packed with excitement, and I’m still buzzing from the incredible conversations and curiosity from everyone who attended!

![Luca Berton presenting at Ansible London Meetup 2024](/blog/conferences/ansible-meetup-london-2024.jpg)

---

## ⚙️ My Talk: Deploying GraphRAG with Ansible

For my demo, I showcased how to use Ansible to:

- Automate the deployment of **Neo4j**
- Connect and configure **OpenAI’s LLM (ChatGPT)** for GraphRAG
- Showcase how **graph-enhanced retrieval** can be orchestrated smoothly using **Ansible Playbooks**
- Highlight how this integrates into modern GenAI pipelines

It was awesome to see the audience's response and the thoughtful follow-up questions afterward!
