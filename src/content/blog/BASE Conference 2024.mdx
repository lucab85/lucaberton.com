## 🌍 BASE Conference 2024: Scaling with Values

Held at the **Social Impact Factory in Amsterdam**, BASE Conference 2024 brought together thought leaders, founders, and change-makers to explore how values-driven innovation can shape the future of business and technology.

Themed *"Connect, Collaborate & Scale"*, the event’s **Elevate Pillar** featured a standout panel:  
**"How to Implement AI for Small Businesses While Staying True to Values"**.

## 🧠 Panel Focus Areas

The conversation centered around **ethical and practical AI implementation for SMEs**, with opening questions like:

- How can small businesses compete with AI-powered tech giants?
- What role should policymakers play in avoiding AI monopolies?
- How can ethical AI be a competitive advantage for smaller players?
- What advice helps businesses turn AI from a concept into real value?

### Key Discussion Themes:
- **Levelling the Digital Playing Field:** How SMEs can compete without falling for AI “snake oil”  
- **Niche Markets & Specialization:** Using AI to outmaneuver larger competitors  
- **Ethical AI as a Differentiator:** Building trust and transparency into your product  
- **Future-Proofing:** Upskilling, adaptability, and long-term values alignment

---

## 🎯 Highlights from Each Panelist

- **Katherine Bustos:** Stressed the importance of **inclusive, ethical AI** frameworks, even for resource-limited SMEs  
- **Luca Berton:** Demonstrated how tools like **ChatGPT and Ansible** enable small teams to scale operations while staying human-centered  
- **Max Van Riel:** Shared how AI can **automate compliance** with building regulations while maintaining transparency  
- **Sebastian Owen:** Emphasized strategy, advising SMEs to **future-proof AI adoption** with governance, innovation, and integrity

---

## 🧩 Panel Format (45 minutes)

1. **Welcome & Framing the Discussion** – AI for SMEs: potential and pitfalls  
2. **Panelist Introductions** – Showcasing each speaker's perspective  
3. **What is an SME?** – Framing the context: fewer than 250 employees, resource constraints, big ambitions  
4. **Opening Question:**  
   *“What’s the biggest challenge SMEs face when implementing AI while staying true to their values?”*  
5. **Deep-Dive Questions:**  
   Each speaker addressed real-world concerns about ethics, automation, trust, and adaptability  
6. **Audience Q&A** – Lively interactions on implementation barriers and transparency  
7. **Closing Reflections** – How to take AI from **conceptual to actionable**, without compromising values

---

## 🔍 Why It Mattered

BASE Conference 2024 proved that **ethical, open innovation isn’t just for large enterprises**. From empowering builders with automation to embedding transparency in AI tools, the panel made it clear:  
➡️ **SMEs can lead the AI revolution by staying true to what matters most.**
