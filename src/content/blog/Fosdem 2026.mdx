## 🚀 FOSDEM 2026 — Still Buzzing!

Just back from **FOSDEM 2026** in Brussels — and the energy was incredible. **8,000+ open source minds**, **65 beers**, **1,013 talks**… and enough hallway track conversations to fuel a year's worth of ideas.

---

## 🔧 RISC-V & Open Silicon

This year, I had the chance to connect deeply with the teams from **DeepComputing** and the **RISC-V International Foundation**, and I must say: the momentum around **open silicon** and **custom compute** is real.

Seeing the energy in the **RISC-V devroom** and chatting about **hardware/software co-design** reminded me how far open innovation has come — and where it's going.

### 💡 Key Takeaways

- **RISC-V is getting real traction** for AI at the edge
- **Hardware is getting more open** — and that unlocks a new class of innovation
- Still amazed by how **strong and welcoming** the open source community is

---

## ☁️ Enterprise AI & Cloud Native

From **Red Hat OpenShift AI** to **edge GPU orchestration**, it was also great to swap stories from the field — especially how we're applying these architectures **at scale** for enterprise-grade AI/ML workloads.

Topics that came up again and again:
- Multi-tenant GPU orchestration
- MLOps pipelines at enterprise scale
- Infrastructure as Code for AI workloads
- Kubernetes-native ML serving
