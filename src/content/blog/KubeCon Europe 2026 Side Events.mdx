## 🎉 KubeCon EU 2026 Side Events — The Unofficial List

> **Tip:** Most events are free but require registration. Grab your spot early — they fill up fast!

## 🗺️ Survival Tips

1. **Register early** — most side events cap attendance and fill within days.
2. **Plan your route** — Amsterdam is bike-friendly, but events are spread across the city. The A'DAM Lookout is a short ferry ride from Centraal Station.
3. **Pace yourself** — with events running from morning till past midnight, pick your battles.
4. **Network intentionally** — side events are often more intimate than the main conf. Come with a goal.
5. **Check back here** — I'll update this list as new events are announced.
