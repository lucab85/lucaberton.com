## What is Root Cause Analysis?

Root Cause Analysis is a strategic approach used to identify the origin of issues rather than simply treating symptoms. It's widely applied across industries—from manufacturing to healthcare and IT—to:

- Reduce recurring problems  
- Improve operational performance  
- Strengthen quality assurance practices

Mastering RCA is a valuable skill for professionals in **project management**, **operations**, **quality control**, and more.
