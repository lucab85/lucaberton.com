## Course Description

In today’s tech-driven world, the ability to solve IT issues and clearly explain technical problems is invaluable. This Coursera course, led by **Luca Berton**, gives you both: expert troubleshooting techniques and effective communication strategies.

Whether you're new to the field or refining your skills, you’ll learn to:
- Diagnose software and network issues  
- Use real-world case studies to apply new methods  
- Communicate clearly with clients and stakeholders  
- Build confidence in delivering technical solutions  

> 🛠 **No prerequisites needed** — just curiosity and a desire to level up your support skills.

---

## Course Outcomes

By completing this course, you'll be able to:
- Use diagnostic tools to analyze and solve complex IT issues  
- Monitor and troubleshoot networks using advanced techniques  
- Manage client expectations through clear, empathetic communication  
- Apply structured processes to reduce downtime and increase reliability
