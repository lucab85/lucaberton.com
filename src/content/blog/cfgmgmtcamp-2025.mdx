## Configuration Management Day @ CfgMgmtCamp 2025

Held in Ghent 🇧🇪 as part of the legendary **Configuration Management Camp**, this year’s event spotlighted **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)**, **DevOps culture**, and **automation leadership** — with voices from across the open source spectrum.

## Featured Presentation: Luca Berton at CfgMgmtCamp 2025

![Luca Berton presenting at CfgMgmtCamp 2025](/blog/conferences/CfgMgmtCamp-2025.jpg)

### **Automating AI-Powered Graph Databases with Ansible: A Neo4j GenAI Case Study**

Luca Berton opened Configuration Management Day 2025 with a cutting-edge session focused on automating Neo4j-based **GenAI environments** using Ansible.

In this practical deep-dive, Luca demonstrated how Ansible can:
- Automate **Neo4j Ops** across cloud and hybrid environments
- Manage GenAI workloads and **infrastructure orchestration** efficiently
- Integrate **event-driven automation** into data pipelines
- Use callback plugins and inventories for performance tuning at scale
