## 🇨🇿 DevConf.cz 2023 — Brno, Czech Republic

[DevConf.cz](https://www.devconf.info/cz/) is one of the largest open source community conferences in Europe, hosted annually in Brno by Red Hat. This year, the event was a celebration of **DevOps**, **automation**, and the **future of open source infrastructure** — and Luca Berton was on-site to capture it all.
