Choosing the right tool matters. Here is my comparison based on real production experience.

## Quick Comparison

Both tools have their strengths. The right choice depends on your team, infrastructure, and requirements.

## Key Differences

The most important differences come down to:
- **Learning curve** — how quickly can your team be productive?
- **Ecosystem** — community, plugins, integrations
- **Scalability** — how well does it handle growth?
- **Cost** — open source vs enterprise licensing

## When to Use Each

### Use Case 1: Small Teams
For small teams, prefer the simpler tool with less operational overhead.

### Use Case 2: Enterprise
For enterprise, consider support contracts, security features, and compliance certifications.

### Use Case 3: Multi-Cloud
For multi-cloud, choose the tool with broader platform support.

## My Recommendation

There is no universally "better" tool — only the right tool for your context. I have used both in production and they each excel in different scenarios.
