Who said KubeCon is just about pods and clusters?

It is time for **KUBEROKE**. 🎤🎶

## The ultimate KubeCon after-after party

If you are at KubeCon EU in Amsterdam this week and feeling the need to belt out some tunes, the incredible [Lian Li](/blog/kubecon-2026-lian-li-platform-engineering/) is your go-to host.

Kuberoke is not just karaoke — it is the Cloud Native community letting loose after days of deep technical sessions, hallway track conversations, and expo booth demos. It is where Kubernetes maintainers sing power ballads, SREs perform dramatic renditions of 80s hits, and platform engineers discover hidden vocal talents they never knew they had.

<lite-youtube videoid="ehhJwH9JyAI"></lite-youtube>

## What to expect

**🎤 The Main Event** — A massive karaoke party going late into the night. Whether you are a shower singer or secretly semi-professional, the stage is yours. The crowd is supportive, the energy is electric, and the song selection is legendary.

**🛋️ The Chill-Out Room** — Not a fan of singing? No worries at all. There is a dedicated chill-out room where you can escape the noise, hang out, and chat with friends in a relaxed vibe. Some of the best KubeCon conversations happen here — away from the conference buzz, with zero pressure.

## Details

- **📅 When:** Tuesday evening, March 24th — Doors open at 9 PM
- **📍 Where:** Between Reguliersdwarsstraat and Amstel, Amsterdam
- **🎟️ Tickets:** Required for entry — grab yours before they sell out

## Why Kuberoke matters

The Cloud Native community is one of the most vibrant in tech, and events like Kuberoke are a big reason why. It is easy to forget that behind every pull request, every Helm chart, and every incident response is a human being who deserves to have fun.

Kuberoke strips away the titles, the company logos, and the conference badges. On that stage, a CNCF ambassador and a junior DevOps engineer are equals — both nervously reading lyrics off a screen, both getting cheered on by a room full of strangers who just became friends.

That is community. That is what makes KubeCon more than a conference.

## See you there

If you are in Amsterdam for [KubeCon EU 2026](/blog/kubecon-europe-2026-side-events/), do not miss this. It is the highlight of the social calendar, and Lian Li makes sure it is an unforgettable night of music, drama, and genuine human connection.

I will be there. Come say hello — and maybe we can do a duet. 🎶
