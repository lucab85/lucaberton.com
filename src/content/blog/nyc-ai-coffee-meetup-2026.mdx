## Why Coffee Conversations Matter

Some of the best technical insights I have gained came from informal conversations — not conference talks, not blog posts, but a 45-minute coffee chat where someone shared a war story about a production GPU cluster failure, or described how they solved multi-model serving at scale with a creative [KAI Scheduler](/blog/gpus-take-flight-red-hat-summit-2026/) configuration.

The AI infrastructure space is moving so fast that the best knowledge is often six months ahead of any published documentation. Real conversations with practitioners are the fastest way to learn.
