Platform engineering is about building golden paths that make developers productive without sacrificing governance.

## The Internal Developer Portal

Every platform team needs a self-service portal. Backstage by Spotify is the most popular choice:

- **Service catalog** — what exists and who owns it
- **Templates** — spin up new services with guardrails
- **TechDocs** — documentation as code
- **Plugins** — extend with CI/CD, monitoring, cost

### Getting Started

```bash
npx @backstage/create-app@latest
cd my-backstage-app
yarn dev
```
