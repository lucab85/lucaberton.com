## About the Book

*Practical RHEL AI* covers everything you need to deploy and scale AI on Red Hat Enterprise Linux:

- **InstructLab** — fine-tune Granite models with your own enterprise data
- **GPU acceleration** — hardware selection, multi-GPU training strategies, vLLM inference optimization
- **Production deployment** — SELinux hardening, container isolation, monitoring and observability
- **RAG implementation** — retrieval-augmented generation patterns for enterprise knowledge bases
- **Scaling** — from single-node development to multi-node production inference

Whether you are a platform engineer setting up AI infrastructure or a developer building your first enterprise AI application, this book gives you the hands-on recipes to go from zero to production on RHEL AI.
