## Quick Decision

| Scenario | Choose |
|----------|--------|
| Kubernetes-native GitOps | **Crossplane** |
| Multi-cloud IaC standard | **Terraform** |
| Continuous drift reconciliation | **Crossplane** |
| Largest provider ecosystem | **Terraform** |
| Platform engineering abstractions | **Crossplane** (Compositions) |
| Team already knows HCL | **Terraform** |

Crossplane reconciles continuously (like a Kubernetes controller). Terraform runs on-demand. The best teams use both — Terraform for initial provisioning, Crossplane for ongoing management.

See the full comparison at [Crossplane vs Terraform 2026](/blog/crossplane-vs-terraform-2026/).

## My Recommendation

Use **Terraform** for broad IaC needs. Add **Crossplane** when you want Kubernetes-native infrastructure management with continuous reconciliation. [Book a consultation](https://calendly.com/lucaberton/) to design your IaC strategy.
