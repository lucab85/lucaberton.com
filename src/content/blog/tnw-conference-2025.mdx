## 1. Why TNW still matters

**TNW Conference** bills itself as *“the most talked‑about tech festival in Europe.”* After two sun‑splashed days on Amsterdam’s NDSM wharf, I get why: it’s equal parts business conference and music‑festival energy. With **4,500+ tech enthusiasts**, **2,000+ startups**, and **675+ investors** roaming repurposed shipyards, the collision density for serendipity is off the charts.

## 2. Festival vibes & first impressions

* Open‑air venue ringed by shipping containers → instant street‑art aesthetic.
* Lanyards color‑coded by persona (startup, investor, corp) → networking on easy‑mode.
* Coffee trucks, silent‑disco headsets, and a DJ booth next to the main stage remind you this *isn’t* your average expo hall.

## 3. Content highlights (my cheat‑sheet)

| Stage | Talk | Why it stuck |
|-------|------|--------------|
| Vision | **“The Heart of Tech”** by *Thomas Wolf (Hugging Face)* | Practical roadmap for open‑weights models + EU policy. |
| Growth | **“From Seed to Series B in 24 Months”** panel | Raw fundraising war stories — slides full of real numbers. |
| Impact | **“AI Storytelling”** by *King Willonius* | Funny, sharp, and a masterclass in live prompting.

> 💡 **Hallway takeaway:** Investors asked less about TAM, more about *path to profitability & AI defensibility*.

## 4. Startup & partner pavilions

* **Hong Kong Pavilion**: low‑tax pitch + cross‑border funding routes. <img src="/blog/conferences/tnw2025/hk_pavilion.jpg" alt="Chatting at the Hong Kong startup pavilion" width="640" height="360" />

## 5. Personal sprint log

1. **Speed‑dates with VCs**: 6 × 15‑minute pitch/feedback loops; two follow‑ups booked.  
2. **Book swap** → snagged *Break Through Banking* on the pop‑up stairs overlooking the venue.  
3. **Orange volunteer tee on Day‑2**": helped direct foot‑traffic, scored backstage speaker chatter.  
4. **Boat‑afterparty on the IJ**: networking turned sunset cruise; still amazed at the density of founders in one spot.
